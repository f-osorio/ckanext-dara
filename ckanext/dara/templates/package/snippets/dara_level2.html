Metadata Level 2
Support findability of your data in disciplinary portals

By adding metadata on this level your dataset can be discovered in disciplinary portals, by search engines or in data bases. Also linking data to (already published) publications is enabled. You have to fill in at maximum 25 metadata fields with additional required subfields. This amount of information is not sufficient to link your datasets to other outputs of your scientific work or to those of your institution.
